Duffing Attractor

The Duffing attractor is a 2-dimensional strange attractor produced by the following equation:

xn+1 =xn+yndt yn+1 =yn+ gcos(wdt) -dyn +axn -bxn3
  • a = linear stiffness
  • b = amount of nonlinearity in the restoring force
  • d = damping
  • g = amplitude of the driving force (0 if no driving force)
  • e = angular frequency of the periodic driving force
