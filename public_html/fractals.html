I became captivated by the beauty and variety of chaotic attractors while searching for a test case for learing DirectX 11 compute shaders. Their varying orbits are a nice demonstration of the underlying dynamics found in other fractal types. My goal here is to animate them in "real time" with varying parameters to produce video of their motion.
