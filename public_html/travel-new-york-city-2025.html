New York City 2025

We visited NYC for the weekend and saw Central Park, the Empire State Building 86 and 102 floors, Chinatown, Times Square, the Brooklyn Bridge, the 9/11 Memorial, and the Occulus. We even managed to take in The Book of Mormon.

Trip highlights are the accordion player under a bridge in Central Park and lunch at Rico Bagles

Next time We'll have to visit The Statue of Liberty, Ellis Island, Governor's Island, The American Museum of Natural History, the Greenline, and the Main Library. Hopefully we'll also get a chance to learn more about architecture, history, geology.
